Certificat racine :

openssl genrsa -out ca.key 2048
openssl req -x509 -new -key ca.key -out ca.pem -days 365 -nodes
openssl x509 -in ca.pem -out ca.crt -inform pem -outform der


Certificat client :

openssl genrsa -out Alka.key 1024
openssl req -x509 -new -key Alka.key -out Alka.csr
openssl x509 -x509toreq -days 365 -in Alka.csr -signkey Alka.key -out Alka.req


Signer le certificat du client :

openssl ca -config openssl-ca.txt -infiles Alka.req
